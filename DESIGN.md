# Design System: KRONVÄRD — Stockholm Atelier

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
**Philosophy:** Stockholm Atelier — Warm Quiet Luxury.
The atmosphere is warm, gallery-like, and trustworthy. Inspired by the curated boutiques of Östermalm. We use ivory tones instead of harsh whites, and rich walnut darks for depth. Typography is the primary design element.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
*   **Ivory Warm (#FAF8F5):** Main background — warm, easy on the eyes.
*   **Soft Cream (#F2EDE8):** Surface color for product cards and image containers.
*   **Dark Walnut (#1C1714):** Primary text and buttons.
*   **Stone Grey (#6B635B):** Secondary text, descriptions, and metadata.
*   **Warm Silver (#D9D3CC):** Borders, dividers, and secondary button borders.
*   **Aged Brass (#8B7355):** Accent color — used sparingly for links and sale prices.

## 3. Typography Rules
*   **Primary Font:** Inter — used for all body text, headings, and UI elements.
*   **Mono Font:** Roboto Mono — used exclusively for prices, SKUs, and technical specs.
*   **Headers:** Light weight (300), wide letter-spacing (0.08em), uppercase.
*   **Body:** Regular weight (400), 15–16px, line-height 1.7.
*   **Brand Name:** Semibold (600), 15px, tracked uppercase.
*   **Product Brand Labels:** 11px, medium weight, tracking 0.12em, uppercase, Stone Grey.

## 4. Component Stylings
*   **Primary Button:** Dark Walnut bg, Ivory text, no border-radius, uppercase, tracked, semibold.
*   **Secondary Button:** Transparent bg, Warm Silver border, Dark Walnut text. Hover: border darkens.
*   **Cards:** Flat, no shadows. Surface background (#F2EDE8). Hover: whisper shadow + subtle image scale.
*   **Inputs:** Square corners, Warm Silver border, Ivory background.

## 5. Layout Principles
*   **Grid:** Clean 3-column grid for products.
*   **Margins:** Generous — max-width 1440px, px-6 content container.
*   **Hero:** Centered text, large font-light heading, separated trust bar below.
*   **Language:** Swedish throughout (sv locale).
